============================
PRODUCT INFORMATION
============================

SmartDesk Pro is an electric height-adjustable standing desk designed for home and office use.
Available colors: Matte White, Graphite Black, Walnut Wood finish.
Dimensions: 140cm x 70cm surface, height range 70cm to 120cm.
Weight capacity: 80kg.
Motor: Dual-motor lift system, whisper-quiet at 45dB.
Memory presets: 4 programmable height presets.
Warranty: 2 years on frame and motor.
Price: Rs. 32,999 (standard) | Rs. 39,999 (premium finish)

Q: How do I program the height presets?
A: Adjust the desk to your desired height using the up/down buttons. Hold the memory button (M) for 3 seconds until you hear a beep, then press preset button 1, 2, 3, or 4 to save that height. Press the preset number once to recall it.

Q: What if the desk stops moving?
A: First check the power cable is connected. If unresponsive, perform a reset: unplug for 30 seconds, replug, then hold the down button for 10 seconds until the desk lowers completely and beeps. Contact support if the issue persists.
